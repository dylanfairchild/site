South Korea
Seoul Arrival
After our long cross-Pacific flight we arrived in Seoul exhausted. We started by finding the hotel, then headed out to Kwanjang Market to try some authentic Korean food.
Seoul Day 2
Our first full day in Seoul we decided to soak up some of the history of Seoul and hit palaces, hanok villages, and Buddhist temples. Of course, then we found our way to the "food street"!
Seoul Day 3
We could not hit all the shrines and palaces yesterday, so for today we set out for Jongmyo Shrine and Gyeongbokgung Palace. We also climbed to the top of N. Seoul Tower to get a birds eye view of the city (and some chicken and beer)!
